Welcome to Preview Week of ICML 2020!

Welcome to the thirty-seventh International Conference on Machine Learning, ICML 2020! This week is the preview week for ICML 2020 - as a registered attendee, you have access to all materials for accepted papers, tutorials and invited talks starting this week: this way, you can watch talks on your schedule and use the time during the conference (July 12-18) for interaction with other conference participants.

The schedule for release of materials is:

  • July 6, 2020: all main conference papers, supplements, and talk videos, together with calendar links to the poster sessions for all main conference papers.
  • July 9, 2020: Invited talk videos, and calendar links to Q&A+Panel discussion sessions of invited talks

     Tutorial videos, and calendar links to Q&A sessions of tutorials

As a preview of the programming that's available at ICML this year, we have:

  • Sunday July 12: EXPO day. This is an opportunity to interact with corporate sponsors[a], participate in panels, view demonstrations of machine learning systems, and participate in EXPO workshops.
  • Monday July 13: Tutorials & Awards. There are 10 outstanding tutorials, each of which will be presented at two different time slots. The tutorials are all 3hr long, with four breaks for Q/A and live text-based chat with the presenters as the videos are played back.
    There is also the presentation of the 2020 Awards, including the (previously announced)     Test of Time award and talk by the winners (presented twice), as well as the announcement of the Best Paper Awards: at 23:00 AOE July 12 and again at 09:00 AOE July 13.
  • Tuesday--Thursday July 14--16: Main Conference. Although videos for main conference papers will have been available since July 6, the three days of the main conference are a time for you to attend Q&A sessions with the authors to discuss work and meet new people.
    On each day of the main conference, there will also be     an invited talk and panel discussion, again at two different times (which vary by day).
  • Thursday July 16: ICML Business Meeting & Townhall. Every year there is a business meeting in which ICML organizers and ICML President discuss the state of ICML, and solicit feedback from the community. This is an opportunity to learn what goes on behind the scenes, and also ask questions and have your voice heard. Two times: 00:00-01:00 AOE and 11:00-12:00 AOE.
  • Friday--Saturday July 17--18: Workshops. You can find more details about workshops here, though note that the details of the schedule may change up until July 13.
  • Throughout:
  • Socials & Mentoring. There will soon be an announcement of social events and mentoring sessions that will take place throughout the conference.
  • Affinity Group Workshops and Events. There are several events during the workshop: Women in Machine Learning (July 13), LatinX in AI (July 13) , Queer in AI (every day), and New in ML (July 13).
  • Sponsor Halls. You will have the opportunity to visit virtual "booths" hosted by sponsors. This is a great place to find job or internship opportunities, and to interact with researchers who work at organizations that have sponsored ICML.
