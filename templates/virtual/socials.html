Socials at ICML 2020

Welcome to the social page. Please find a list of socials and mentor sessions below. We also provide some conference-wide options to interact. Please always respect others and adhere to the ICML Code of Conduct

    is a virtual space to meet and hangout at virtual ICML in a virtual 2D world at GatherTown. Please check the Getting Started Guide (.pdf) for setting it up. Enter ICML-Town here
